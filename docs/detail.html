Featured Case Study

Editorial UI

Rejecting digital noise for the rhythmic, intentional pacing of a physical art publication.

Overview

Editorial UI is a conceptual design system that prioritizes composition over conventional UX patterns, creating a gallery-like browsing experience for long-form content.

Tech Stack

  • React.js
  • Tailwind CSS
  • Framer Motion

Role

Lead Designer & Developer

The Loss of Pacing

Modern interfaces suffer from a relentless density. By maximizing "functional density," we've lost the rhythm that makes reading a physical magazine pleasurable. Our challenge was to re-introduce the pause.

Intentional Voids

We used scale as an architectural marker. By creating massive gaps between headlines and body copy, we created a "compositional tension" that naturally guides the eye without relying on heavy UI borders or navigation bars.

01

Tokenized Voids

A strictly enforced spacing system that prioritizes white space over content density.

02

Typographic Rigor

The massive contrast between Newsreader Display and Manrope Sans defines the information hierarchy.

03

Silk Gradients

Subtle, non-distracting 135-degree gradients that provide a tactile, paper-like finish.
